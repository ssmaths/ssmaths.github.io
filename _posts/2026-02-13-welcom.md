An incident handler’s journal acts as a record for every observation, decision, and technical action taken during a security crisis. It transforms chaotic forensic data into a structured chronological narrative, ensuring no critical detail is lost. This document serves as the definitive source of truth for post-incident analysis, legal accountability, and the continuous improvement of a team's defense strategies.
